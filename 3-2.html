Chapeltown Karate Club

About

Chapeltown Karate Club was formed by Sensei Gary Baker 6th Dan in July 2001 @ Newton Hall, Chapeltown.

Gary who is Ex British Army, has over 35 years experience in Martial Arts and is a firm believer in Discipline to get the Best out of Students.

At CKC we teach Life Skills such aa

  • Self Discipline
  • Motivation
  • Fitness
  • Balance
  • Flexibility and Co-ordination
Everyone is welcome. We haev classes for children (4 yrs and above) and classes for adults. We belive age is just a number and reguarly teach mums, dads and even grandparents.
