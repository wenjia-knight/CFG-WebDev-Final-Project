Chapeltown Karate Club

Chapeltown Karate Club was formed by Sensei Gary Baker 6th Dan in July 2001 @ Newton Hall, Chapeltown. Gary who is Ex British Army, has over 35 years experience in Martial Arts and is a firm believer in Discipline to get the Best out of Students.

At CKC we teach Life Skills, Self Discipline, Motivation, Fitness, Balance, Flexibility, Focus & Co ordination and Much more for Children, Boys & Girls, 4 years of age upwards including Adults. We have Men & Women (Moms & Dads), even Grandads who are actively training with us regularly and prove that age is just a number.
